About Me

I’m Heng — a licensed Cambodian tour guide with over 10 years of experience. I started guiding in Siem Reap and have been based in Kampot for more than three years. Popular Tours

Bokor Mountain

Half Day

Visit the famous Bokor Mountain’s to see the famous historical sites that date back to the time of the French, as well as the stunning views from the mountain top.

Visit will include:

Lok Yeay Mao statue

29‑meter monument on Bokor Mountain honoring Yeay Mao, a Neak Ta guradian figure revered by locals.

Black Palace (Damnak Sla Khmao)

Royal retreat from the late 1930s; weathered hilltop ruins with sweeping views.

Popokvil Waterfall

Two‑tier cascade in Bokor National Park; especially misty and scenic in the rainy season.

Wat Sampov Pram (Five Boats Pagoda)

Hilltop Buddhist pagoda named for five boat‑shaped rock formations; founded in the 1920s.

Old French Church (Bokor Church)

Stone Romanesque‑style Catholic church built in the 1920s at Bokor Hill Station; an atmospheric icon.

Bokor Mountain

Full Day

Upgrade the Bokor tour to a full day to see the countryside highlights of Kampot, including our famous pepper farms, caves, salt fields and more!

Visit will include:

La Plantation pepper farm

Guided walk through organic Kampot pepper plots with tastings.

Salt fields

Coastal salt pans where seawater evaporates to produce Kampot sea salt.

Secret Lake

Reservoir near Kampot, quiet rural scenery; loop road circles the shore.

Ancient caves (Phnom Chhnork)

Limestone cave with a 7th‑century Hindu brick temple dedicated to Shiva.
