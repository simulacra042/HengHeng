About Me

I’m Heng — a licensed Cambodian tour guide with over 10 years of experience. I started guiding in Siem Reap and have been based in Kampot for more than three years. I love sharing the mountain air on Bokor, the flavors of Kampot pepper, and quiet river sunsets. My tours are small, friendly, and flexible — with plenty of photo stops.

  • Hotel pickup for all tours (Kampot town)
  • Clear communication and careful driving
  • Flexible timing on request
